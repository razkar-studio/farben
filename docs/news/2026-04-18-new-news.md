# Farben has a news section now

You're looking at it!

Changelogs are great for "what changed in 0.17.2" but terrible for
"here's the silly bug I spent a weekend chasing and what I learned."

This is the place for that kind of thing, release notes with context,
design rationale, the occasional retrospective when something I shipped
turns out to have been a mistake.

Posts will probably be irregular. I'm not going to pretend I'll write
weekly. When something's worth saying, it'll show up here.

Thanks to everyone who's tried Farben, opened issues, checked it out, 
or just starred the repo! It genuinely keeps me going. More soon!

Cheers, RazkarStudio
